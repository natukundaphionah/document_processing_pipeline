# Omugasho gw'obwegyese
- One of the reasons why parents don't read with their children at home is because they don't value education.
- They don't believe that educating their children is important.
- Use the facts below while facilitating a discussion that will be triggered by the Forum Theatre Drama Skit on the Value of Education, or while holding one-on-one discussions with parents on the Value of Education:
1. Okushomesa omwaana waawe nikimuyamba kwega kushoma n'okuhandiika.
2. Okushomesa omwaana waawe nikimuyamba kutunga obukugu oburamubasise kutunga omurimo n'amagara marungi.
3. Okushomesa omwana waawe nikyongyera aha migisha ye y'okutuunga nyensya nungi.
4. Uganda n'ensi yoona aherikuza, nikiza kuba kigumire omuntu atashomire kutunga ekyokukora n'okwebisaho.
5. Omwana waawe aine obubagabe bw'okutunga obwegyese.
Turebeka ngu twatungira abaana beitu ebitabo ebishemeire eby'okushoma kandi tushomera hamwe omuka.

# Emigasho ya abazaire
- Parents don't know that they have a role to play in the reading of their children.
- They believe the education of their children is a responsibility of teachers.
- Parents don't know that they have to read with their children at home.
- They also don't know that an uneducated parent can read with her/his children at home.
- Use the facts below while facilitating a discussion that will be triggered by the Forum Theatre Drama Skit on the Role of the Parent, or while holding one-on-one discussions with parents on the Role of the Parent:
1. Abaana abatarikwega kushoma omumyaka ena erikubanza aha eishomero nikibagumira kwega kushoma kandi nibabaasa n'okuruga omwishomero.
2. Abaana nibeega gye kandi jubajuba okushoma baba bakiri omu kyokubanza, ekyakabiri, ekyakashatu n'ekyakana.
3. Nobuwakuba otarikumanya kushoma, nobaasa kuhwera abaana baawe kwega kushoma orikukora ebi hanu: Kubaganira ebigaano, kweshongora nabo, kukyebera omubitabo byaabo nabo, kubabuuza okubasiiba aha eishomero, kubabuuza ebibashoma aha eishomero, kubabuuza kukworeka ebibakuunda aha eishomero, kubahurikiriza barikushoma ninga barikugana, kureeba nabo ebishushani omubitabo, kubayamba kweega kwatura ebigambo n'amaziina gebintu bitari bimwe nabimwe, hamwe n'okuzaana nabo.
4. Okuganiira, okuzaana, n'okweshongora n'abaana baawe nikibayamba kwega kushoma ahakuba abaana nibashomagye ebigambo ebibarikubaasa kwatura.
5. Okuhurikiriza abaana baawe barikushoma nikikubasisa kubahwera kwega kwatura ebigambo ebibari kwatura Kubi.
6. Okuhereza abaana baawe obwiire n'okubahurikiriza nikyibagaruramu amaani kushoma n'okureeba okushoma nk'ekyomugasho. Nikibareteera n'okukunda okushoma.
7. Torikwetenga kuba n'omanya kushoma kwetegyereza abaana baawe ebibarikushoma ninga barikukugambira. Oyine kutunga obwiire okabahurikiza kyonka.
8. Okuhereza abaana baawe obwire n'okubahurikiriza, nikyoreeka ngu n'ofayo aha kushoma kwaabo. Abaana nibakunda kushemeza. Kwokwereka ngu n'ofayo aha kushoma kwaabo, nibaza kuguma nibagyezaho kukushemeza batyo begyeremu.
9. Nikirungi kumara edakiika makumi abiri ninga makumi ashatu buri eizooba orikushomera hamwe n'abaana baawe omuka.
10. Tungayo omwanya omunju yaawe ninga ahantu hoona omuka yaawe nka omu muryango, omukiyuungu, ahibaraza ninga ahansi y'omuti omukibuga aha orikubaasa kushomere hamwe n'okuzaana n'abaana baawe.

# Obwire
- Parents are too busy. They work the whole day. They are tired most of the time. This will stop them from reading with their children at home.
- Parents also give their children a lot of work after school. This will also deny parents a chance to read with their children.
- Use the facts below while facilitating a discussion that will be triggered by the Forum Theatre Drama Skit on "Time", or while holding one-on-one discussions with parents on "Time":
1. N’obworaabe oyine bingi eby’okukora, gyezaho otunge akaire oshomera hamwe n’abaana baawe omuka.
2. Nikirungi kumara edakiika makumi abiri ninga makumi ashatu buri eizooba orikushoma, kugaaniira, kuzaana, kugana ebigano, n’okweshongora n’abaana baawe n’okubahurikiriza omuka.
3. Waaba oyine eby’okukora bingi munonga, nobaasa kukoresa akaire koona akorikutunga kushomera hamwe n’abaana baawe. Ondijo aha b’eka yaawe (nka mukuru ninga munyanya, ishenkazi ninga ishento, nyinenkuru ninga ishenkuru w’abaana baawe) nabaasa kukuyambaho kushomera hamwe n’abaana baawe.
4. Ninga nobaasa kushomera hamwe n’abaana baawe aha rwamukaaga ninga saande.
5. Ningashi nobaasa kushomera hamwe n’abaana baawe nyekiro bwanyima y’emirimo yaawe.
6. Nobaasa kushomomera hamwe n’abaana baawe waba nokora emirimo yahaka nk’okuteeka, okwozya ebintu, okunaaba, okwozya emyenda, okuhiinga, okutaha amaizi, okushenya enku, orikuza kugura ebintu omukatare ninga omu maduuka, okwaara ekitanda, n’ebindi.
7. Eky’omugasho n’okumara akaire orikushomera hamwe n’abaana baawe omuka.
8. Hereza abaana baawe emirimo mikye omuka kugira ngu mutunge obwiire mushomere hamwe omuka.
9. Nobasa kuhwera abaana baawe kukora emirimo kugira ngu mutungye akaire k’okushomera hamwe omuka.

# Enkoragana y'omuzaire n'omwaana
- Parents cannot read with their children at home because children fear parents.
- Parents beat, insult and abuse their children. This makes children dislike their parents. Hence, they cannot read together.
- Parents believe that punishing their children is the effective way to discipline them and make them behave well.
- Some parents also fear that if they play, laugh and interact with their children, the children will disrespect them.
- Use the facts below while facilitating a discussion that will be triggered by the Forum Theatre Drama Skit on "Parent-Child Relationship", or while holding one-on-one discussions with parents on "Parent-Child Relationship":
1. Okuzaana nabaana baawe, kuganiira nabo, kusheka nabo, kweshongora nabo n'okubanywaana, tikirikubaretera bakurabyamu amaisho.
- Okwihaho nikikuretera kuhikaana nabo nokukwatanisa gye nabo.
- Nikibaretera nokukunda bakukunda.
2. Okuteera, okujuma, n'okubonabonesa abaana baawe, tigwo muringo oguhikire okubahana.
3. Nobaasa kuhana abaana baawe otabafubiire. Omwana waawe yaba ari omunshobi, gamba nawe, omuhabure. Otamutera, otamukabukira, ninga okamujuma.
4. Okugira enkoragana nungi nabaana baawe nikikuyamba kugamba nabo, kweshongora nabo, kuzaana nabo, n'okushomera hamwe nabo omuka.
5. Okuganiira, okuzaana, okugana n'okweshongora n'abaana baawe nikibayamba kwega kushoma ahabwokuba abaana nibashoma juba ebigambo ebi barikubaasa kugamba.
