# Writing or speaking?

- Ci = chi
- Ba= wa (we forgive you!)
- Ba is honorifics. It precedes a name to denote respect
- Example: Ba Sharon if I am way older than you
- Ba Mbuya (Grandma), Ba Shikulu (Grandpa), Ba tata(dad), Ba mayo (mum)
